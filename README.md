## Description

Care Scribe is a website that allows care takers and medical professionals to track their patients and patient information. Through Care Scribe users can create patients, add appointments and medications, track their appointments through Care Scribe's calendar, and write down notes for each patient. We make care easy!

## Using Care Scribe

 <details >
  <summary> Navigating Care Scribe</summary>
    Navigating is easy on Care Scribe, the nav bar allows you to move to any page to add information or view your patients and appointments. The details of any information added - patient details, mediation or appointments details - can be accessed bye clicking it. 
</details>

  <details >
  <summary> Adding patients and patient information</summary>
   Start by creating a patient and add their appointments and medications to their page. All appointments are now available on the calendar! All patients and patient information can be edited or deleted using the buttons located at the bottom of the page or below the corresponding information.
</details>
